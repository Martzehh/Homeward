A Beacon of Hope

In a world ravaged by darkness there is but one city left. Sanctum is the last line of defense against the all-consuming Nether. A beacon of hope for many because of the brave protectors that dwell inside. These ‘Keepers’ devote their lives to defending Sanctum so that others may live free of the corruption that lies beyond the city walls. Unfortunately, even the strongest have trouble resisting the temptations of darkness...

Sanctum has Fallen

Exiled for his desire to use the Nether’s power for his own gain, Ghesi sought revenge on the Keepers. He ventured forth into the darkness outside the city walls to make a deal with the demigod Bozod. In exchange for the destruction of Sanctum, Ghesi was given powers far greater than those of the warriors that protect the city. Knowing they would do anything to protect their city, Ghesi used his forbidden powers to rip the Keepers far away from Sanctum leaving them helpless as their beloved city fell.

Oct 2021 Pre-sale
Homeward Founders Pre-Sale

You're here and you are early! As an early Homeward supporter you deserve a few perks. That's why we decided to roll out the Homeward Founders Sale. This is a 3 week limited sale with some serious benefits. Founders ticket holders recieve things like free NFT claims, airdrops or physical merchandise. You even have a chance to design your own Homeward hero and recieve lifetime royalties on all sales!

It doesn't matter how many tickets we sell, after the 3 week period is up any remaining unsold tickets will be burned forever.

Nov 2021 Launch
Collection is live!

We want the Homeward NFT collection to be something truly unique. To do that we are taking our time and making sure every element of the launch will be perfect. You can expect all the things that have become standard to recent NFT launches such as: Rarity sniper, Rarity tools, Community giveaways, Community treasury established, Physical merchandise, and Collaborations.

Post-launch Arcade
Rolling up our sleeves...

Immediately following launch, work on the Homeward arcade continues. Anything in the arcade will be exclusive to Homeward NFT holders. The concepting for this is complete, as it's an idea one of our founders had many years ago - you may have even seen some of the pixel art sneak peaks on our Twitter! We expect this to be complete relatively quickly following launch (3 - 5 months).

Post-launch Story
Expanded Story

Homeward isn't just a run-of-the-mill NFT project. We are setting out to build a universe and every universe needs a great story. What better way to make this story come to life than to have an animated short!? Just like the arcade, the storyboard for this video was created a very long time ago! Using the funds raised we will have the animated short 'The Fall of Sanctum' come to life. This will act as an excellent point to on-board new community members and open the doors to future animated work done in the future.

Long-term Beyond
Establishing the metaverse

We have many plans for the Homeward metaverse but we can't get too far ahead of ourselves. While we are laser focused on the short/medium term goals previously posted, know that the foundation for the Homeward metaverse has been put into place. After we've hit our previous goals we will allocate resources to do some truly incredible stuff like: push out regular Homeward manga chapters, purchase land in The Sandbox, and even one day establish a Homeward DAO!
